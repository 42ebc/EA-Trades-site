The Rules We Live By

Tsukuyomi operates on a set of hard rules. These aren't suggestions — they're the foundation of every profitable trader in this community.

01

Max 3 Trades Per Day

Quality over quantity. If you've taken 3 trades, you're done for the session — win or lose. Overtrading is the fastest way to blow an account.

02

Stop After 2 Consecutive Losses

Two losses in a row means the market isn't aligning with your thesis. Step away, reset, and protect your capital for tomorrow.

03

Zero Revenge Trading

Chasing a loss is how small losses become catastrophic ones. The market doesn't owe you anything. Accept the loss and move on.

04

Patience Over Action

The best trade is sometimes no trade. We wait for the setup to come to us — we don't force entries out of boredom or FOMO.
